## Priority Levels

- **P0 (Critical)**: Blocking users, security, data loss
- **P1 (High)**: Important features, high-impact bugs
- **P2 (Medium)**: Nice to have, medium-impact
- **P3 (Low)**: Polish, low-impact

**Management**: PM reviews weekly, keeps <50 items, archives old P3s
